VachanOnline

VachanOnline.com is a humble yet ambitious pilot project to provide unrestricted access to biblical resources in Indian languages. It is an initiative of Friends of Agape (FOA), USA. The material in this site currently is not complete and are only samples of the kind of content that would eventually be made available. The material is free to use but not for redistribution in an other format/platform.
